Biography

Formed in the beginning of 2000 in the cold north of Finland, and shaped into the current form in 2005, Vortech combines both fast and slow approaches to metal with the futuristic scenes of industrial machinery working overdrive.

The debut album Conclusion (2006) proved to be the lasting foundation in style and gained interest all around the world. Quickly followed by one of the band's most popular albums, Wasteland (2007), bringing in some hard-hitting speed into the mix, solidified the musical style that you know and enjoy. A third album, Deep Beneath (2008), was published not long after, bringing in a bit of progressive touches.

Not soon after, the fourth album, Posthumanism (2009), proved to be a hit among fans and garnered world-wide interest across platforms. At this time the band also took the show on the road and performed a series of live shows, all the while composing the fifth album Devoid of Life (2012) and planning for testing a whole new electronic approach in The Occlusion (2014), and merging the fast metal and extremely electric styles finally on ...of What Remains (2015).

Currently the newest album - eighth in total - The Shadow Presence (2022) was a combination of cooperation with fans and new guests, fusing all the styles so far into a single tight package of fast, futuristic and electrified metal. And with a new album nearing release, it will soon be time to enter the dystopian universe once more!
