A look to the future

20 Jul 2016

The machine is ever moving forward, and you can hear some roars in this video clip: There is no album title yet, but there are a lot of songs done/in progress already. Some of them might be familiar from FAWM, but those tracks will be of course tweaked to suit the style. Here's the working titles in alphabetical order: Actuator Heart Aural Disconnect Binary Evolution Cybernetic Warrior Filtered from Existence From Biodeath to Persistence Gothenbass Hinge Upon Devastation Hunter Manipulation Gear Photon City Revolution Mechanism Source Corrupt The Cybernetics The Evolved Ones The Fading Humanity The Flesh Responds The Thought Machinery What Makes Us Cyborgs Yes, it's quite far in the process already! The release will probably land somewhere in early 2017 or so, depending on how the vocal recordings will go.
