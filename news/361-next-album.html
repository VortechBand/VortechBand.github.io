Next album

9 Jul 2022

Not a surprise to some, but yes, it is indeed time to start the process that will end with the release of our next album! There is no working title for the 9th full length album yet, but the 11 songs are very much fully demoed and clocking in at around 50 minutes. Now it is time to start fleshing them out, adding all kinds of tricks, and writing the lyrics. Also known as preproduction. All things going smoothly, it should be out sometime in 2023... PS the work towards a website refresh is going on little by little, as time allows, and should please mobile users quite a bit!
