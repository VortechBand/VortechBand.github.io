It is time to start recording the album!

All demo vocals are now ready, and that of course means it is time to start recording the album! Given the nature of the songs, it should prove to be a quick endeavour! Hang tight and check our Twitter, Facebook, or this website to catch the latest news! Altogether there are 18 songs to be recorded, of which 14 will be on the album and the remaining four will be on an EP. Both will be released around the same time, so you'll have a nice year with Vortech! The release date depends on how quickly we can conclude the recording and finish the mixing, but usually they have not taken too long... Stay tuned for more info about the album name and titles.
