Jesperi-klubi in Tornio, FI on 24 Nov 2001

Juha Untinen - Guitar

Tero Konola - Drums

Samuli Tarvainen - Bass

Setlist

  1. Loppusyksy
  2. Endless Stairs
  3. The Sentence Is Death

Report

Our very first gig, in a local youth festival with many other local bands playing a short set each. For this gig we got one of our friends to join in on bass on short notice, but everything went well. We rehearsed for a couple of weeks before the show. There is a recording of the show as well, which has the last two songs in full.
