Report

Very moody and cool show in a venue with plenty of personality and personalities! This time the show was again with our traditional setup and we had plenty of fun playing for a new audience a bit more due south from our usual grounds. We also recorded the show but the quality turned out fairly low. The setlist was cool and well rehearsed, and all and all a great gig with plenty of attendance despite the unusual location!
