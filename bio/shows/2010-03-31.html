Report

Probably one of our coolest shows, in the metal capital of Finland! We had again plenty of friends listening, even all the way from Spain! The show went great and it was a blast to play with the audience well into the music. This was also the very first time we played Neurovirus live, long before it was actually released in April 2012. It had Matti's original vocals, of course, so quite a unique performance.
