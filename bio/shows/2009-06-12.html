Report

The first of many gigs this year with our actual songs. Exciting times also as it was the first time playing with drums coming from a multitrack source, and also reamped second guitar and bass! But everything went really smoothly and with just two actual people we still managed to create quite a ruckus! The tracklist features all our favourites at that point and the venue was really nice, with some friends in attendance as well as lots of other people. Maybe around 50-60 people total, for this small-ish club.
