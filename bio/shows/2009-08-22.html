Report

Only after one show, it was already time to play at a festival, of course! In a secluded town south of Oulu, it still managed to gather a lot of people and we had plenty of chats with people prior to the show. Everyone had a blast and the setlist was even faster than the previous show! Had a great time with the other bands as well, and enjoyed the venue to the fullest!
