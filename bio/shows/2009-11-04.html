Report

A very familiar venue from playing with some other bands. Always a great blast playing in this legendary small club! The house was quite full and this time tried out a bit special way to have our backing tracks in the PA - just a simple mp3-player! But even that worked out great and still sounded okay in a very small place. The people seemed to enjoy the show and this time Matti compiled the setlist out of his favourite tracks, making it a little bit different from previous shows.
